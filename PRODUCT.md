# Punto de Venta System

## Users
- Small business owners and retail staff
- Cashiers and sales associates
- Inventory managers
- Business administrators

## Product Purpose
A modern point-of-sale system designed for small to medium retail businesses. Provides inventory management, sales processing, customer management, and business analytics through an intuitive web interface.

## Brand Voice
Professional yet approachable, efficient and reliable. Focuses on simplifying complex retail operations while maintaining a clean, trustworthy appearance.

## Anti-References
- Overly complex enterprise POS systems with steep learning curves
- Outdated interfaces with poor usability
- Systems that prioritize features over user experience

## Strategic Principles
1. Simplicity first - intuitive workflows that require minimal training
2. Performance - fast response times even with large inventories
3. Reliability - offline capability and data synchronization
4. Clarity - clear visual hierarchy and feedback
5. Adaptability - works across different business types and sizes
